Peter Thiel

Peter Andreas Thiel (/ˈtiːl/; born October 11, 1967) is an American entrepreneur, investor, and philanthropist. He is the founder and CEO of Palantir Technologies , a data analytics company. Thiel is also known for his involvement in the tech industry and his investments in various startups.

Early Life and Education: Thiel was born in Frankfurt, West Germany, and raised in the United States. He attended Stanford University, where he earned a Bachelor of Arts in philosophy and a Juris Doctor from Stanford Law School.
